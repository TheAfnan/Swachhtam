## About

Swachhtam is an AI-powered civic engagement platform that enables citizens to report, track, and resolve hyperlocal community issues through intelligent automation, transparent workflows, and collaboration with municipal authorities.

The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence, multilingual accessibility, geospatial mapping, and real-time complaint tracking to make civic issue reporting simple, transparent, and efficient.

---

## Problem

Communities frequently face issues such as potholes, overflowing garbage bins, broken streetlights, drainage problems, water leakages, and damaged public infrastructure. Existing reporting systems often lack transparency, provide limited tracking, and make communication between citizens and authorities difficult.

Swachhtam bridges this gap by providing an intelligent platform where citizens can report issues, monitor every stage of resolution, and actively participate in improving their communities.
